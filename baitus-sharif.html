About This Project

Baitus Sharif is a strategically located mixed-use development that combines the best of residential and commercial spaces. Situated near the Siroil-Dhaka Bus Stand on Siroil-Sagorpara Road, this project offers unmatched connectivity and convenience for both residents and businesses.

The project's prime location ensures excellent accessibility to major transportation routes, making it ideal for families and entrepreneurs alike. Baitus Sharif represents a modern approach to urban living, where work and home seamlessly integrate.
