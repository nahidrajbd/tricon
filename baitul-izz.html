About This Project

Baitul Izz offers a unique living experience in a peaceful and spiritually enriching environment. Located on the north side of Siroil Central Masjid, this residential project is perfect for families seeking tranquility and community living in the heart of Siroil.

The project's proximity to the central mosque provides residents with easy access to daily prayers and community activities, while maintaining a serene atmosphere ideal for family life. Baitul Izz combines traditional values with modern living standards.
