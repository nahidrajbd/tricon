About This Project

Waheed Heights represents the pinnacle of modern residential living in the heart of Siroil, Rajshahi. This completed project showcases Tricon Properties' commitment to quality construction and thoughtful design.

Located at the prestigious Doshor Mandol Mor area, Waheed Heights offers residents easy access to essential amenities, educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and commercial centers. The strategic location ensures excellent connectivity to all parts of Rajshahi city.
