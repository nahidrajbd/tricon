About This Project

Shwapnokunju, meaning "Dream Garden," is a newly launched residential project that offers spectacular views of the Rajshahi Divisional Stadium. Located in the emerging New Bilshimla area of Rajpara, this development combines modern urban living with the excitement of being near one of Rajshahi's premier sports and entertainment venues.

The project is designed for those who appreciate both comfort and convenience, offering residents a unique lifestyle with stadium views, green surroundings, and excellent connectivity to the city's key areas.
