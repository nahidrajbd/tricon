About This Project

Dhrubotara is an ongoing luxury residential project located in the prestigious Uposhahar area of Rajshahi. This development embodies elegance and comfort, designed specifically for families seeking a premium lifestyle in one of the city's most desirable neighborhoods.

Situated at 180/3 Uposhahar, Dhrubotara offers residents the perfect blend of modern amenities and peaceful living. The project is being developed with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that every aspect meets the highest standards of quality and luxury.
